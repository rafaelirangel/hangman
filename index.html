Welcome to the HangMan!

GAME INSTRUCTIONS

Guess the letters in the secret word to solve the puzzle. You can guess a letter by clicking on it.
Game ends when the player either correctly identifies the word or submits 6 incorrect guesses.
Choose play to jump right into the game, or choose 'Enter Your Own' to enter your own choice of word by typing it on your keyboard and press the 'Enter' key to start.

GAME TIPS & STRATEGIES

Use your vowels. Looking at vowels is a good way to start. Since there are only 5, you can fill in some blanks pretty quickly. They can even help you guess the answer straight away. For example, if you try the letter A and the word looks like this ‘_A_A_A’, you might be able to guess straight away that the answer is BANANA just from one letter.

Good Luck and Have fun!
